chiping forn cats

Keep your cat safe, protected, and always identifiable with our professional microchipping service. A microchip is a small, permanent form of identification that greatly increases the chances of being reunited with your cat if they ever go missing. Our microchipping process is quick, safe, and carried out with your cat’s comfort as the top priority. The chip—about the size of a grain of rice—is placed just beneath the skin using a gentle procedure similar to a routine vaccination. Once registered, your cat’s unique identification number is securely linked to your contact details in a national pet database.
